About Yelp

Yelp is a tool that allows you to find locations or business establishments such as restaurants, bars, home services and so on. Once you find the place you are looking for, Yelp will display important information that will help you determine whether this is what you want or not. It will relevant information such as address, contact information, directions, hours of operation, and more. What makes it unique is that it also allows users to post reviews and photos. This is critical because many customers rely on other customers' word of mouth to determine if an establishment is the right for the customer.
